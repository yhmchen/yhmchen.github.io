This is a research project on solar neutrino oscillation. I designed and built a modular numerical framework to simulate neutrino physics surive probability. I also tried to independently search for literature and find and think about feasible topics.

Project Details and Code

  • Project Title: Solar Neutrino Oscillation
  • Project Description: This project studies solar neutrino oscillations from both theoretical and numerical perspectives. I built a modular numerical framework to simulate solar neutrino survival probability. Given only a broad direction, I independently conducted literature review, explored potential observable extensions, and proposed preliminary research concepts. This experience helped me develop the ability to extract meaningful research questions from broad topics.
  • Research Focus: Independently conducted a literature review on MSW effects and parametric resonance, reviewed solar neutrino papers, and explored potential extensions beyond the Standard Model, including dark photon interactions. This helped identify directions for further exploration of a preliminary concept for neutrino-dark photon interactions.
  • Tools / Languages: Python, Jupyter Notebook
  • Code Repository:¬† 2-flavor Solar Neutrino Oscillation
  • Technical Implementation and Rigor: The simulation required independently modeling and solving the complex neutrino flavor evolution equation with spatially varying matter potential (MSW effect) and integrating over the BP2000 solar density profile. As integrity and customizability of numerical solutions are paramount in high-energy physics research, the modular Python framework was implemented using rigorous methods. Validation was performed through consistency checks and cross-verification against known theoretical limits, ensuring the fidelity of the physics results.
