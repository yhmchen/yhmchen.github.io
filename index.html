Yu-Hsin Melissa Chen

I am interested in exploring the different subfileds of physics such as high energy physics and condensed matter physics. Now I am a undergraduate research assistant at Academia Sinica.

My research experience include

  • Topological density wave (Condensed Matter Theory) in Professor Chen-Hsuan's group : Currently focusing on theoretical analysis and numerical simulation of topological properties in these systems.
  • Solar Neutrino Oscillation (High Energy Theory) in Professor Meng-Ru's group : Developed a modular numerical framework to study neutrino physics, including MSW effects and exploring preliminary models for dark photon-neutrino interactions. (The numerical framework is **publicly available on my GitHub**).
  • **LIGO Data Analysis (High Energy Experiment) in Professor Henry's group :** Conducted numerical simulations and systematic parameter scans on publicly available LIGO data, focusing on careful problem identification and data interpretation.
